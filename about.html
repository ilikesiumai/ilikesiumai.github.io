ABOUT ME

About me? I go to King George V School, am currently studying my GCSEs, and plan to study IB and go on to some university in the United States. I hope that I can persue a career that allows me to meet new people, solve problems, make lives better. I can speak both English and Mandarin, but as I live in Hong Kong, that's nothing special. I have danced Ballet since I was five, and am currently Grade 6 RAD. I know a limited amount of Python, HTML, and CSS, if you read the home page, then you would know I made this website during Year 10 Work Experience.

Something I have a huge passion for is art. I've written a lot more about it on my 'Illustrations' page. Art gives us the ability to express ones thoughts and emotions in an abstract form, which in the fast-paced, modern day world, is something I feel we students need. With the structured society we live in today, it is important that not just students, but all of us don't forget how to be creative and think outside-of-the-box. For me, putting a pencil down and being able to scrawl a piece of paper all over with ideas is a breather from the now increasing amount of tests and exams.
